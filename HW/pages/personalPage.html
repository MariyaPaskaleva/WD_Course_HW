The project

The project that I'm intrested in and I want to work on is about a site that contains different videos and information on how to work out at home, or anywhere else in that matter.
There are a lot of videos with great exercises - if only they were gathered at one place. The idea is to be able to find videos with exercises you like to try - low or high impact exercises depending on your mood and physical state, different categories of exercises - aerobical, fitness, yogic.
There will be links to the official pages of the video creators so you can follow them for more content.
